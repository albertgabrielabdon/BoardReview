## Key Features

### Custom Library Management
* **Add via File:** Upload your own `.docx` lecture notes to instantly expand your question list.
* **Manual Control:** Use the **Library Tab** to add individual questions, edit existing entries for better clarity, or remove outdated notes.
* **Full Reset:** Want a clean slate? Use the **Clear Database** option to wipe the prebuilt notes and build a library entirely of your own.

### Dynamic Quiz Engine
* **Customizable Sessions:** Choose the number of questions you want to tackle based on your available study time.
* **Note Specifications:** Filter your quiz to focus on specific modules (e.g., just PSYC 27) or mix them all for a comprehensive challenge.
* **Instant Feedback:** Each question provides an explanation to reinforce your memory immediately after answering.

### Performance Analytics
Watch how well you do in the **Stats Tab**:
* **XP System:** Gain experience points for every correct answer (wow gamified?).
* **Accuracy Tracking:** Take note of your total correct vs. wrong answers to identify which topics need more focus.
* **Inventory:** View the total number of items currently stored in your personal database (currently at 757 preset).
